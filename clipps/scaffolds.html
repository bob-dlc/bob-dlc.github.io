Scaffolds, Supports & Options

CLIPPS implements a particular set of scaffolds, supports, and options in presenting instructional materials to each individual students. The following scaffolds, supports, and options are provided in the demo.

  • Scaffolding Graphical Interpretation
  • Scaffolding for Vocabulary and Background Information Differences
  • Scaffolding Production of Reflective Writing
  • Scaffolding for Organizational Challenges
    • Concept Density
    • Highlighting Critical Features
    • Navigation Supports
